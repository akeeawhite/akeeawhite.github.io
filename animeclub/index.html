ABOUT MCTC Anime Club

MCTC (Minneapolis Community and Technical College) Anime Club was founded by former club president and co-founder Andy Freeman in 2011.It is one of many student organizations supported by MCTC Student Life. Since that time, MCTC Anime Club has helped more than 100 members,both past and present, to appreciate Japanese culture through anime. Meetings are held in the S building at the MCTC campus.

OUR VALUES


Openess

We welcome not only current MCTC students, but alunini and other people in the MCTC community - regardless of gender identity, race, religion or any other background.


Education

In order for our members to positivety appreciate anime, we often view and discuss the various anime titles, as well play games related to anime


Fun

We encourage everyone to part take in our discussion and join in on the events related to our club.
